PAYMENT AND REFUND POLICY

LEGUMBRERÍA Web Platform

Effective Date: April 6, 2026
Owner: LEGUMBRERÍA
Location: Itagüí, Antioquia, Colombia

1. Accepted Payment Methods

LEGUMBRERÍA accepts the following payment methods:

  • Credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, or other authorized providers)
  • Bank transfers (when enabled)
  • Authorized electronic payment platforms
  • Cash on delivery (if applicable and available in the selected area)

All payments must be made in Colombian Pesos (COP) unless otherwise indicated.

The Company reserves the right to modify or restrict payment methods at any time without prior notice.

2. Payment Processing

2.1 Authorization
All payments are subject to verification and authorization by the corresponding financial institution.

2.2 Order Confirmation
An order is considered confirmed only after payment has been successfully processed and verified.

2.3 Fraud Prevention
LEGUMBRERÍA reserves the right to cancel or suspend any transaction suspected of fraud, unauthorized use, or violation of security policies.

Additional identity verification may be required before approving certain transactions.

3. Pricing and Billing

Prices displayed on the Platform include applicable taxes unless otherwise specified.

In case of pricing errors due to technical issues, the Company reserves the right to cancel the order and notify the User before processing.

The User agrees to provide accurate billing information.

4. Refund Policy

4.1 Eligibility for Refunds
Refunds may be granted in the following cases:

  • Payment was successfully processed but the order was not delivered.
  • The delivered product was incorrect.
  • The product was significantly damaged during delivery.
  • The order was cancelled before shipment.

4.2 Non-Refundable Situations
Refunds will not be granted in cases where:

  • The User provides incorrect delivery information.
  • The product deterioration is due to improper storage after delivery.
  • The claim is made outside the established timeframe.

5. Refund Request Procedure

To request a refund, the User must:

  • Submit a written request via the official contact email.
  • Provide order number, payment proof, and description of the issue.
  • Attach photographic evidence (if applicable).

Refund requests must be submitted within 48 hours of product delivery.

6. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 5 to 10 business days.

Refunds will be issued using the original payment method whenever possible.

Processing times may vary depending on the financial institution.

LEGUMBRERÍA is not responsible for delays caused by banking institutions.

7. Partial Refunds

In certain situations (e.g., partial damage or incomplete orders), the Company may grant a partial refund proportional to the affected products.

8. Right to Refuse Refund

The Company reserves the right to deny refund requests that:

  • Do not meet eligibility requirements
  • Contain false or misleading information
  • Show signs of abuse of the refund policy

9. Legal Compliance

This Payment and Refund Policy complies with applicable Colombian consumer protection regulations.

10. Policy Updates

LEGUMBRERÍA reserves the right to modify this Shipping Policy at any time. Updates will be published on the Platform and become effective upon publication.

RETURNS POLICY

LEGUMBRERÍA Web Platform

Effective Date: April 6, 2026
Owner: LEGUMBRERÍA
Location: Itagüí, Antioquia, Colombia

1. Purpose

This Returns Policy establishes the conditions under which customers may return products purchased through the LEGUMBRERÍA platform, in compliance with applicable Colombian consumer protection regulations.

2. Eligibility for Returns

Returns may be accepted under the following circumstances:

  • The product delivered does not match the order placed.
  • The product was delivered in visibly damaged condition.
  • The product shows quality defects at the time of delivery.
  • The product is expired at the time of receipt.

All return requests must be submitted within 48 hours of delivery.

3. Non-Eligible Products for Return

Due to the perishable nature of agricultural goods (legumes, vegetables, fruits, herbs, cereals), returns will not be accepted when:

  • The product deterioration is due to improper storage after delivery.
  • The return request is made outside the 48-hour period.
  • The product has been partially consumed or altered.
  • The damage is caused by mishandling by the customer.

4. Return Request Procedure

To initiate a return, the customer must:

  • Contact LEGUMBRERÍA via the official email or customer service channel.
  • Provide the order number and detailed explanation of the issue.
  • Submit photographic evidence when applicable.

The Company reserves the right to request additional information to evaluate the claim.

5. Inspection and Approval

All return requests are subject to verification by LEGUMBRERÍA.

If the return is approved, the Company may offer:

  • Replacement of the product, or
  • Refund in accordance with the Payment and Refund Policy.

The decision will be communicated within 3 to 5 business days after reviewing the request.

6. Return Logistics

If a physical return of the product is required:

  • The Company will coordinate pickup, or
  • The customer may be instructed to return the product to the physical store (if applicable).

Shipping costs for approved returns due to company error will be assumed by LEGUMBRERÍA.

7. Right of Withdrawal (Derecho de Retracto)

In accordance with Colombian consumer protection laws, the right of withdrawal may apply except for:

  • Perishable goods
  • Goods that cannot be returned due to their nature

Given the nature of agricultural products, the right of withdrawal may be limited in accordance with legal exceptions.

8. Abuse of Policy

LEGUMBRERÍA reserves the right to deny return requests in cases of:

  • Repeated misuse of the return system
  • False claims
  • Evidence of fraud

9. Policy Modifications

LEGUMBRERÍA may update this Returns Policy at any time. Updated versions will be published on the Platform and become effective immediately.
